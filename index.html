Indian restaurant · Hartsdale, NY

North to south on one plate

NH 44 Indian is an Indian restaurant in Hartsdale, Westchester County, NY, inspired by National Highway 44 — India's longest highway. Our menu travels the full route from Kashmir to Kanyakumari with tandoor-fired kebabs, slow-cooked curries, biryanis, and vibrant street-food favourites.

Dine in for lunch or dinner, order takeaway and delivery online, or request catering for corporate events, weddings, and parties. We serve vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-friendly dishes alongside chicken, lamb, seafood, and pork specialties. Signature plates include butter chicken, chicken tikka masala, lamb rogan josh, Hyderabadi-style biryanis, paneer tikka, and small plates like samosas and onion pakora.

Our dining room features funky truck-art murals and Indian street culture — the first of its kind in Westchester County. Read our full story or explore the menu.

Frequently asked questions

Do you take reservations?

Yes. Reserve a table online or call (914) 574-5262. Walk-ins are welcome when tables are available.

Yes. Order online for pickup or delivery. We are also on UberEats, DoorDash, and GrubHub.

Yes. Our menu includes vegetarian, vegan, and many gluten-friendly Indian dishes. Please tell your server about any allergies.

Do you offer catering?

Yes. We cater corporate events, weddings, birthdays, and parties in Hartsdale and Westchester. Request catering or call us to plan your menu.
