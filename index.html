Indian restaurant · Hartsdale, NY

North to south on one plate

NH 44 Indian is the best Indian restaurant in Westchester, NY — an Indian restaurant in Hartsdale inspired by National Highway 44. Our menu travels from Kashmir to Kanyakumari with tandoori chicken, chicken tikka masala, garlic naan, biryani, kebabs, and slow-cooked curries.

Dine in for lunch or dinner, order takeaway and delivery online, or request catering for corporate events, weddings, and parties. We serve vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-friendly dishes alongside chicken, lamb, seafood, and pork specialties. Signature plates include butter chicken, lamb rogan josh, Hyderabadi-style biryanis, paneer tikka, and small plates like samosas and onion pakora.

Our dining room features funky truck-art murals and Indian street culture — the first of its kind in Westchester County. Try the Xpress Thali lunch special, read our full story, or explore the menu.

Questions

Frequently asked questions

What is the Xpress Thali lunch special?

Xpress Thali is our weekday Indian lunch special — Monday–Friday, 12:00 PM–4:00 PM, dine-in only. Choose Vegetarian ($18), Chicken ($20), Lamb ($25), or Seafood ($25) with Curry, Korma, Tikka Masala, or Tandoori. Every thali includes appetizer, rice, naan/roti, dal of the day, saag paneer, vegetable sabji, raita, pickle & pappad. See Xpress Thali on the menu.

Do you take reservations?

Yes. Reserve a table online or call (914) 574-5262. Walk-ins are welcome when tables are available.

Do you offer delivery and takeout?

Yes. Order online for pickup or delivery. We are also on UberEats, DoorDash, and GrubHub.

Do you have vegetarian or vegan options?

Yes. Our menu includes vegetarian, vegan, and many gluten-friendly Indian dishes. Please tell your server about any allergies.

Do you offer catering?

Yes. We cater corporate events, weddings, birthdays, and parties in Hartsdale and Westchester. Request catering or call us to plan your menu.
