A Culinary Journey Through India

NH 44 Indian brings delicacies from across India, representing an eclectic north to south culinary experience. Named after India's longest highway, our menu, developed by Jeevan Pullan & Roshan Balan, offers modern interpretations of classics and popular Indian roadside food.

From succulent kebabs to rich curries and street vendor foods, we use traditional Indian spices. Our interiors, designed by Thida Kongthai, feature vibrant murals inspired by Indian trucks, creating a transformative atmosphere that celebrates India's street culture, a first of its kind in Westchester County.
